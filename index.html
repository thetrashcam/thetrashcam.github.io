About Us

TrashCam is a product that helps you reduce food waste costs by learning your customer preferences using what they throw away.

Motivation

Why save food?

Roughly one third of the food produced in the world for human consumption every year — approximately 1.3 billion tonnes — gets lost or wasted.

Every year, consumers in rich countries waste almost as much food (222 million tonnes) as the entire net food production of sub-Saharan Africa (230 million tonnes).

Solution

What We Do.

Capture Data

Using a custom built camera unit for your dining location, we capture images of the food wasted.

Image Analysis

Using machine learning, we analyse the images collected and return data of food wastage to dining managers.

Data Usage

We use the said data to provide insights into major waste sources and possible changes needed in the menu pattern for waste minimization.
